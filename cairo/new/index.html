How Much Interest in Rentals around the World Since 2004?

The seven most popular rental categories - car, house, apartment, equipment, company, uniform and jewelry. The size of each bubble is determined by the search index. In this project, except for geographic maps, the maximum search index is 100 and the minimum search index is 0.

Proportional Search Interest Since 2004 Worldwide by Rental Category

Any change in interest for rentals since 2004?

Interest in all rental categories reached their peak in 2004 and fluctuated until 2018. These downward trends might suggest that people have increasing disposable incomes and they prefer to buy commodities instead of renting them.

What the Geographical Overview Looks Like in the U.S.?

The interest in car rental is high in popular tourist states such as Nevada, Hawaii, Alaska and Florida. Texas has the higherinterest in house rental, while New York and surrounding states have high interest in apartment rental and jewelry rental. Midwest states have high search index in uniform rental. With regard to equipment and company rental, no single area or state seems to be dominating.
