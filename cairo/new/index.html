What does the changs of search interest in Rental like Since 2004?

Three animations are presented to compare the change through years and categories. Search interest for all rental categories reached their peak in 2004 and follow down to 2018 in general due to the increasing salary and living standards. Car rental search interest reaches the peak in 2004 and remain in the bottom from 2012 to 2015. Lastly, it goes up till now. The reason might be there is an increasing number of people starting to travel around since 2015. From the second animation, the similar trends also exist in apartment and equipment. Surprising, the search interest for house decrease until 2013 and goes up until now, maybe the housing price is too high since 2013 and people cannot afford it. However, there is no big changes in search interest about uniform and jewelry.
